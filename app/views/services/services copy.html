The heart of our company is orchestration. We are organized and well versed regarding all types of events, and would like to share that experience with you.

As there is a scale for both budget and need of our services, we've separated our skills into several packages to be used as guidelines. All experiences can be customized to meet your needs.

You have ceremony, location, and vendors selected but need assistance in orchestration. We create your wedding day schedule, manage your rehearsal, ceremony, and serve as contact to your vendors.

In addition to the Simply Sweet package, we will wow your family and friends by designing and planning an unforgettable wedding rehearsal and dinner; setting the tone of your wedding weekend.

Need help executing and managing all the details? We'll help you design the wedding of your dreams and find the perfect location for both the ceremony and reception. We'll connect with the best vendors and handle all the logistics.

From start to finish, we'll select the perfect locations, guide you through vendor selection, and manage every detail right down to your wedding celebration brunch, so you can relax and enjoy the wedding of your dreams with your family and friends.
